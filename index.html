Lassonde Engineering Society

University is about more than just getting a degree; it is about getting an education. Much of the difference lies in your involvement in the life of the university — activities such as university governance, sports, social clubs and, as an engineer, the Engineering Society. Together with all the remaining clubs at Lassonde, an active Engineering Society is vital for students and faculty alike. So get involved and broaden your university experience! – Richard Hornsey, Associate Dean for Lassonde School of Engineering.
